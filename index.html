Frequently Asked Questions

1.. The Sadhana has two parts: a Japa in the evening and a Yagna the next morning.
‍
2. When you perform your first Yagna, take a vow or Sankalpa declaring the purpose of your Sadhana. An in-app prompt will guide you.
‍
3. Every form of Devi is invoked with a specific Mantra in Japa and special ingredients in Yagna.
‍
4. If, for some reason, you are unable to perform the Yagna on a particular day, simply repeat that day’s Japa and continue your Sadhana.
‍
‍5. In line with the Vedic tradition, set aside a small monetary offering or Dakshina daily. You can also choose to offer Dakshina at the beginning of your Sadhana.
‍
‍6. You don't require initiation, permission, or any other intervention to do the Sadhana.
‍
7. Anyone over the age of 12 can do the Sadhana (including those who menstruate).
